Our Story

If we had to tell you one thing about us... it would be that we're so thankful. Thankful that God brought us together. Our Journey started back in engineering days when we fight like cats and mouse . We have seen each other grow in life achieving career goals , professional aims , fighting with the odds , learning from our own and each other's mistakes and at last realising how love can make even simple things so beautiful in life .

A thousand mile distance , our professional lives in different time zones in different cities , sometimes crazy mess in lives - everything was Made easy because we were the 'always here for you' support in each other's lives.

Now, almost a decade of knowing each other and surviving successfully, we are ready to embark on the journey of our TOGETHER FOREVER, we are super excited and seek the love and blessings of our loved ones.
